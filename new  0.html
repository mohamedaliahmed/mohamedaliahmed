    Wiki Freeze Today  

	                  The wiki freeze is tonight Be sure to have all of your project information on the
	                  2009 iGEM wiki by 11:59pm EDT.you will not be able to make any edits to the
	                  wiki after that time.
      
	 Also due:

	        * Judging form
		* Project and part documentation
		* DNA submission 

	Update: there were some wiki loading issues leading up to the wiki freeze.

    Submit your judging form  

	
       You can submit your team's judging form! the forms are due on
       October 21 at 11:59pm, EDT.you may edit the form up until the deadline,
       at which point the wiki will freeze and you will not be able make any change.
	 			 
       Note: Software tool teams now have a specialized version of the judging form 
       that reflects the medal requirements for software teams.

    Wiki Freeze & Judging Form  

	 
      There is only ONE week left until the wiki freeze! Be sure to finalize all of your
      information on the iGEM wiki and the Registry before the deadline so that the judges can
      review your project.
      
      the judging form is also due on October 21 at 11:59pm, EDT. the form will be 
      released shorty so keep checking 2009.igem.org for updates.

     show off your team spirit!  

	   
     the jamboree is less than a month away, and although you must be busy with
     preparations, you should also think about your team t-shirts. you're representing your team
